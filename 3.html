Playstation 3

Specs

Dimensions: 290mm × 60mm × 230mm

CPU: 3.2-GHz Cell Broadband Engine

GPU: RSX “Reality Synthesizer” @ 500MHz

RAM: 256MB XDR Main RAM + 256MB GDDR3 VRAM

I/O Output: Hi-Speed USB (USB 2.0) X 2

Optical Drive: KES 410a


Extra Information:

The Playstation 3 is a home gaming console that was released by Sony in 2006. It become the rival to the xbox 360 with both competing to get deals with game companies to try and get more customers. This is also part of the seventh generation of gaming consoles to hit the world of gaming. It was the first console to play blu-ray movies making it more family based.
